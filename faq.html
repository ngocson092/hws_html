A Brazilian wax is the removal of all the hair in your private regions – that includes the labia (if you're a woman) and peri-anal areas as well. At your request, we can even give you a variation called the "playboy wax", where a shape or strip is left on the front! It is different from a bikini wax in that the latter only includes removal of hair that would be visible within the bounds of a swimsuit bottom.

A vajacial is a skincare treatment for women's intimate parts. The 30-minute service treats ingrown hairs, removes dead skin cells, smooths bumps around bikini line, prevents acne, helps with hyperpigmentation, all to maintain healthy and hydrated skin in the vaginal area. First, we assess, steam, and cleanse your skin, then we exfoliate and perform extractions if needed. It is finished off with a hydrating mask and moisturizer. This treatment is appropriate for all skin types, including sensitive skin, and we recommend booking the service 3 days after a wax.

Brow lamination is a noninvasive, semipermanent that alters the direction of your eyebrows, similar to microbalding but with no pigment added to your brow. It adjusts the natural brow pattern and achieves a fuller look. The procedure takes about 30-45 minutes. You walk out with healthy fuller-looking brows.

Typically, eyebrow lamination lasts four to six weeks.
